Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

- To understand what a growth mindset is I think it would be good to go through the opposite first “fixed mindset”.
- A fixed mindset is something when someone believes that their skills, talents and traits are fixed. What you know and what you are capable of will not change hence, you will not be able to learn anything new.
- A growth mindset, on the other hand, is when you take learning and challenges as opportunities. You believe that failure is an opportunity to learn and grow. People with a growth mindset (like myself!) focus on progress and process to stretch their existing abilities.
- It is hugely relevant to learning coding although I have never dealt with coding in my life I still believe I can suss it and change my career in the end.

- The code I wrote for one of the JS challenges was not working and I could not figure out why!!


In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

How will you integrate a growth mindset into your learning journey?
