Identity and Values

My core personal values

Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and the consequences of your action.

- In my previous job where I worked as QA/R&D personnel there was a situation when everyone was preparing for a new product launch with a tight deadline. However, an error was made in the production during the manufacture and one raw material was accidentally added ten times its normal quantity. Due to the nature of the production capacity, we could not fix the problem by adding an extra amount of other ingredients to match the raw material that was overly added. The majority of people were hoping to manufacture a few new batches by mixing them with the portion of the compromised batch to save time and cost. However, I did not feel comfortable with such an approach as there is no guarantee that the compromised batch will deliver the even amount of ingredients that were added. Moreover, it was for human consumption so there was a product quality/safety issue associated with this incident. Therefore, I suggested to manufacture a new batch without using the compromised batch. As a result, our product launch did not meet the date and had to get rid of the old batch which resulted in the company the extra cost. However, we were able to maintain confidence in our products.
